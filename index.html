Meet Elsie and Mor.

( Yes, real people ! )

Hi I'm Elsie, the founder of Mor.cakesandpasteries

Chef Sawayi, a dreamer who baked through both triumph and heartbreak. Elsie found herself starting from scratch, now with a newborn baby in her arms and a heart still full of ambition. In one of the quiet moments of motherhood, the name “Mor.” came to her a word that means joy, It was the perfect name for both her brand… and her baby. With passion as the recipe and purpose as the flame, she invited her siblings, each talented, dedicated, and relentless to co-found what is now becoming a food and lifestyle movement. Our goal is to become a powerhouse in the food and beverage industry. This is the start of something bold and beautiful and we are so honored to build it , with butter with fire, and with family.
