Meet Elsie and Mor.

( Yes, real people ! )

Hi I'm Elsie, the founder of Mor.cakesandpastries

Chef Sawayi began with a newborn in her arms and a quiet determination to keep creating. In one of those still moments of motherhood, the name "Mor." came to her, a word that means joy. It felt like the perfect name for her baby and for the brand she was about to build. Mor. is more than cakes and pastries. It's a celebration of family, creativity, and starting something new. Everything she makes is filled with intention, built with butter, with fire, and with family. With passion and purpose, she invited her siblings, each one gifted and all-in, to help bring the dream to life. From home kitchen experiments to growing demand, what started small is becoming something bigger than they imagined.
